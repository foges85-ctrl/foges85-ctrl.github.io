Project Spotlight: Hybrid Architecture Management

The Intel to Silicon Connection Challenge

One of the most interesting challenges in my lab was managing a legacy **Intel-based Mac** from a newer **Apple Silicon (M-series) Mac**. While macOS Screen Sharing and SSH are usually seamless, architecture differences can lead to unexpected handshake failures.

Troubleshooting Insight: I initially encountered persistent "Connection Timed Out" errors. Through packet inspection, I realized the Mac's built-in Application Layer Firewall was dropping packets during the management handshake.
