Resolving Firewall Bottlenecks: Intel & Silicon Mac

Beyond the hardware, a lab is about solving real configuration hurdles. In this project, I documented the process of managing legacy Intel hardware from an Apple Silicon host while maintaining a strict security posture.

The Fix: Rather than disabling the firewall, I configured service-specific exceptions for TCP 5900 (VNC) and TCP 22 (SSH). This ensured that management traffic could pass between architectures without leaving the systems vulnerable.
