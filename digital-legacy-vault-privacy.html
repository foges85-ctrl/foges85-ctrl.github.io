Digital Legacy
Vault Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Digital Legacy Vault app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created by Sean Fogarty (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a paid service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed with privacy as its core principle. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included
  • No network requests are made by the Application
  • No account creation or login is required
  • No advertising is served within the Application

All information you enter into the Application — including emergency contacts, final wishes, and document locations — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework with iOS Complete File Protection encryption. This data never leaves your device.

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your data or provide features.

Local Storage and Security

Your vault data is protected by the following security measures:

  • Face ID / Touch ID biometric authentication is required to access the Application
  • All data is encrypted at rest using iOS Complete File Protection
  • The Application's data store is explicitly excluded from iCloud backup
  • The Application locks automatically whenever it enters the background

Because all data is stored locally on your device, the Service Provider has no ability to access, recover, or delete your vault data. If you delete the Application, all data is permanently removed from your device.

Third Party Access

The Application does not share any data with third parties. No data is transmitted to external services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or any other third party for any reason.

The Service Provider may disclose information only as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process. However, as no user data is collected or stored by the Service Provider, there is no data to disclose.

Children

The Service Provider does not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. The Application does not collect any personally identifiable information from any user, including children.

If you have questions about your child's use of the Application, please contact the Service Provider at contact@fogartyholdings.com.

Data Retention Policy

All data entered into the Application is stored locally on your device and is under your full control. The Service Provider does not retain any user data. To delete your data, simply delete the Application from your device. All vault data will be permanently removed.

Security

The Service Provider has designed the Application from the ground up with security as a core principle. All data is encrypted at rest, protected by biometric authentication, and excluded from cloud backup. The Application makes no network connections and has no ability to transmit your information to any external party.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to the processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact the Service Provider via email at contact@fogartyholdings.com.
